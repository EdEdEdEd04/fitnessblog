A healthy lifestyle has a different things under it, including a nutritional diet, daily workout, quality sleep and thinking positive. When I was just starting to get a healthy lifestyle I experienced the difficulty in changing my bad habit into a new and advantageous way. I started first to discipline myself and adjust my attitude to form my consistency in life. The discipline and consistency is very important, once you master it everything will follow. The information in the left side are the example of my healthy living, it just a simple daily routine but a powerful practices. When we do all the necessary elements to have a healthy lifestyle, our lives are going on the right path. So, now you have to do is to start and change, once you choose to live a healthy life, it lasts all your life. It’s not only helps you live longer but also better and less prone to sickness and diseases. NUTRITION

HEALTHY BODY, HEALTHY LIFE

Nutrition is simply the process of getting from our food what we need for health and growth. Proper nutrition is one of the most essential elements to being healthy and living a long life. Our diet plays a major role in deciding how healthy we are and how well our body functions. Some information in the left side are typically for those who are gaining muscles and getting physically fit. For me, you don’t have to follow exactly what the needs in men’s nutrition, just add more vegetable and fruits with the source of protein in your plate. In my case I just follow what I can do in men’s nutrition and for me I achieve the nutrition I need for maintaining my form. ”Start where you are, use what you have, do what you can.” You can eat what you want but you have to be careful and in moderate, because anything in excess is bad. If you have no budget to do the men’s nutrition, you can start in avoiding or lessen various food that are harmful to your body. Just do a little bit because without a healthy diet, you could be increasing your risk of chronic diseases. “Our food should be our medicine and our medicine should be our food.”

WORKOUT

NO PAIN, NO GAIN

Exercise, stretching and other recreational activities are very important to a person’s health and it’s all mainly for cardiovascular fitness. All of these can improve your health, body and life. There’s a lot of benefits you can get exercise. Some people thinking about that they need to go to the gym to perform all of the exercises but for me there’s a lot of way to get fit without going to the gym. On the left side I have listed some of my full body home workout routine. Jogging, jumping jacks and squats to improve my lower body, and pushups, pull ups, burpees and planks to improve my upper body. I don’t have much time so I decided to do it in a short period of time, setting up and reducing the time and repetition without affecting the intensity and impact of these home workout in my body. In my case I do it 3x a week and I don’t let a week go by without doing all of these. Discipline and consistency is the key, once you have that you’ll not notice the change happening to you. Applying proper nutrition is the one of best thing when you exerting much effort to your body.

  • Squats 4 sets | Planks 2 sets
